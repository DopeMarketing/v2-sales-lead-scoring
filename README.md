# V2 - Sales Lead Scoring

## Overview
An automated sales lead scoring and assignment platform that imports leads from HubSpot, scores them based on email domain, phone number, and requested items, then intelligently assigns them to sales reps with SMS notifications. The system uses AI to enhance lead qualification and maintains analytics in Google Drive.

## Problem
Manual lead scoring and assignment creates bottlenecks, inconsistent follow-up, and missed opportunities from high-value prospects not being prioritized properly

## Target User
Sales teams and sales managers who need to efficiently process, score, and distribute leads across their team

## Features
- Automated lead scoring with customizable weighting
- Smart lead assignment based on score thresholds and rep availability
- Twilio SMS notifications for high-value lead assignments
- HubSpot lead import with automatic tagging
- AI-powered lead qualification chatbot integration
